# Tutorial Outline: AI Ethics and Safety

**Category**: AI Era
**Level**: 0 (Seed)
**Estimated Time**: 6-8 hours

## Learning Objectives

- Understand key ethical challenges in AI development and deployment
- Identify bias, fairness, and transparency issues in AI systems
- Apply responsible AI practices to projects
- Navigate privacy and consent in AI applications
- Engage thoughtfully in AI ethics discussions

## Section 1: Foundations of AI Ethics

**Concepts**:
- Why AI ethics matters
- Key ethical frameworks (consequentialism, deontology, virtue ethics)
- Stakeholders in AI systems
- Ethical principles for AI (beneficence, non-maleficence, autonomy, justice)

**Skills**:
- Apply ethical frameworks to AI scenarios
- Identify stakeholders affected by AI
- Articulate ethical concerns clearly

## Section 2: Bias and Fairness

**Concepts**:
- Types of bias: data, algorithmic, human
- Fairness definitions and tradeoffs
- Disparate impact and treatment
- Historical bias in training data

**Skills**:
- Identify bias sources in AI systems
- Evaluate fairness across different definitions
- Mitigate bias in datasets and models

## Section 3: Transparency and Explainability

**Concepts**:
- Black box vs interpretable models
- Explainable AI (XAI) techniques
- Right to explanation
- Model cards and documentation

**Skills**:
- Assess model transparency
- Create model documentation
- Explain AI decisions to stakeholders

## Section 4: Privacy and Data Rights

**Concepts**:
- Data collection and consent
- Differential privacy
- Data minimization
- Right to be forgotten

**Skills**:
- Implement privacy-preserving techniques
- Handle personal data responsibly
- Comply with privacy regulations (GDPR, CCPA)

## Section 5: AI Safety and Alignment

**Concepts**:
- AI alignment problem
- Specification gaming and Goodhart's law
- Robustness and adversarial examples
- Long-term AI safety concerns

**Skills**:
- Identify misalignment risks
- Design safety mechanisms
- Test for robustness

## Section 6: Social Impact and Responsibility

**Concepts**:
- Automation and job displacement
- Amplification of inequality
- Dual-use technologies
- Environmental impact of AI

**Skills**:
- Assess social impact of AI projects
- Design for inclusive outcomes
- Consider broader consequences

## Section 7: Governance and Regulation

**Concepts**:
- AI regulation landscape
- Industry self-regulation
- Ethics review boards
- Accountability mechanisms

**Skills**:
- Navigate regulatory requirements
- Participate in ethics reviews
- Implement accountability measures

## Section 8: Responsible AI Practice

**Concepts**:
- Ethics throughout AI lifecycle
- Stakeholder engagement
- Continuous monitoring
- Incident response

**Skills**:
- Conduct ethics assessments
- Engage diverse stakeholders
- Monitor deployed systems

## Capstone Project

**AI Ethics Framework and Case Studies**

Develop comprehensive ethics analysis toolkit:

1. **Ethics Assessment Framework**:
   - Checklist for AI projects
   - Stakeholder analysis template
   - Risk assessment matrix
   - Mitigation strategy guide

2. **Case Study Analysis** (5+ cases):
   - **Facial recognition bias**: Amazon Rekognition, Clearview AI
   - **Hiring algorithms**: Amazon recruiting tool
   - **Content moderation**: YouTube, Facebook algorithms
   - **Criminal justice**: COMPAS recidivism prediction
   - **Autonomous vehicles**: Trolley problem scenarios

   For each case:
   - Background and context
   - Ethical issues identified
   - Stakeholder impact analysis
   - Alternative approaches
   - Lessons learned

3. **Personal AI Project Ethics Review**:
   - Choose a real or hypothetical AI project
   - Conduct full ethics assessment
   - Identify potential harms
   - Design mitigation strategies
   - Create accountability plan

4. **Policy Recommendations**:
   - Draft AI ethics policy for organization
   - Design ethics review process
   - Create incident response plan
   - Develop stakeholder engagement strategy

5. **Presentation and Debate**:
   - Present ethics framework
   - Defend ethical positions
   - Engage in case study debates
   - Facilitate ethics discussion

**Deliverables**:
- Ethics assessment framework (toolkit)
- 5+ detailed case study analyses
- Personal project ethics review (2000+ words)
- Organizational AI ethics policy draft
- Presentation on AI ethics topic
- Reflection essay on personal learning

## Prerequisites

- Basic understanding of AI and machine learning
- Critical thinking skills
- Willingness to engage with complex ethical questions
- Empathy and perspective-taking ability
- Interest in social impact

- **Technology Ethics Broader**: Beyond AI, general tech ethics
