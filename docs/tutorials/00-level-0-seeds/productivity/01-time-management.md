# Tutorial Outline: Time Management

**Category**: Productivity
**Level**: 0 (Seed)
**Estimated Time**: 6-8 hours

## Learning Objectives

- Understand time as finite, non-renewable resource
- Prioritize tasks using proven frameworks
- Plan days, weeks, and months effectively
- Eliminate time wasters and optimize workflow
- Build sustainable time management system

## Section 1: Time Management Foundations

**Concepts**:
- Time as your most valuable resource
- Time vs energy vs attention
- Maker's schedule vs manager's schedule
- Opportunity cost and prioritization

**Skills**:
- Track time usage honestly
- Identify time leaks
- Value time appropriately

## Section 2: Goal Setting and Alignment

**Concepts**:
- SMART goals framework
- Long-term vs short-term goals
- Goals hierarchy (life, year, quarter, month)
- Alignment and focus

**Skills**:
- Set clear, measurable goals
- Break goals into milestones
- Ensure daily work aligns with goals

## Section 3: Prioritization Frameworks

**Concepts**:
- Eisenhower Matrix (urgent/important)
- Pareto Principle (80/20 rule)
- MIT (Most Important Tasks)
- Time blocking and batching

**Skills**:
- Classify tasks by urgency/importance
- Identify high-leverage activities
- Schedule MITs daily

## Section 4: Planning Systems

**Concepts**:
- Daily planning rituals
- Weekly reviews
- Monthly and quarterly planning
- Calendar vs task list

**Skills**:
- Conduct daily planning
- Review and adjust weekly
- Plan quarters strategically

## Section 5: Execution and Focus

**Concepts**:
- Single-tasking vs multitasking
- Deep work vs shallow work
- Pomodoro Technique
- Context switching costs

**Skills**:
- Work in focused blocks
- Minimize distractions
- Complete deep work sessions

## Section 6: Time Tracking and Analysis

**Concepts**:
- Time audit methodology
- Tracking tools and techniques
- Analyzing time data
- Continuous improvement

**Skills**:
- Track time accurately

## Section 7: Saying No and Boundaries

**Concepts**:

## Section 8: Energy and Sustainability

**Concepts**:
- Energy management not just time

**Skills**:

- Take strategic breaks

## Capstone Project

**30-Day Time Mastery Challenge**

Build and refine personal time management system:

1. **Time Audit** (Week 1):
   - Track every hour for 7 days
   - Categorize activities (work, social, rest, waste)
   - Analyze time usage patterns
   - Identify top 3 time wasters
   - Calculate time allocation vs goals

2. **System Design**:
   - Choose planning system (digital/analog)
   - Design daily planning template
   - Create weekly review checklist
   - Set up time blocking calendar
   - Define personal productivity metrics

3. **Goal Setting and Planning**:
   - Define 3-5 major goals (quarter/year)
   - Break into monthly milestones
   - Weekly objectives that align
   - Daily MITs supporting goals
   - Document goal hierarchy

4. **Implementation** (30 days):
   - Daily planning (5-10 min each morning)
   - Weekly review (30 min Sundays)
   - Time block schedule
   - MIT completion tracking
   - Pomodoro sessions (25 min focus)
   - Daily reflection log

5. **Optimization**:
   - Week 2: Eliminate top time waster
   - Week 3: Optimize deep work blocks
   - Week 4: Refine planning system
   - Compare week 1 vs week 4 time audit
   - Document improvements

6. **Deliverables**:
   - Time audit reports (week 1 and week 4)
   - Personal time management system documentation
   - 30 daily planning logs
   - 4 weekly review reflections
   - Goal alignment visualization
   - Productivity metrics dashboard
   - Final reflection on improvements

**Deliverables**:
- Complete time audit analysis
- Time management system documentation
- 30-day implementation log
- Before/after comparison
- Personal productivity playbook
- Reflection essay on learning
