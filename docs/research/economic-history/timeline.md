# Economic History Timeline

> Chronological journey from ancient commerce to cryptocurrency

This timeline provides a linear historical view of economic development, thought, and practice. Each entry links to detailed topic documents.

---

## Ancient Period (3000 BCE - 476 CE)

### 3000 BCE - Mesopotamian Accounting
- **Cuneiform tablets** record debts, trade transactions
- First known accounting systems in Sumerian temples
- See: [Mesopotamian Commerce](ancient/mesopotamia.md)

### 1750 BCE - Code of Hammurabi
- Babylonian law code includes economic regulations
- Interest rates, debt forgiveness, property rights
- Establishes legal framework for commerce

### 600 BCE - First Coinage
- Lydian electrum coins (gold-silver alloy)
- Standardized medium of exchange
- See: [Money: History & Functions](foundations/money.md)

### 350 BCE - Aristotle's *Politics* & *Nicomachean Ethics*
- Distinguishes "oikonomia" (household management) from "chrematistics" (wealth acquisition)
- Critiques usury and "unnatural" profit-seeking
- See: [Greek Economic Thought](ancient/greek-economics.md)

### 27 BCE - 476 CE - Roman Imperial Economy
- Denarius silver coin becomes standard
- Complex tax system (publicani tax farmers)
- Trade networks spanning Mediterranean to India
- See: [Roman Empire Economics](ancient/roman-empire.md)

### 100 BCE - 1400 CE - The Silk Road
- Trans-continental trade routes linking China, India, Persia, Arabia, Europe
- Exchange of goods, technologies, ideas
- See: [The Silk Road](ancient/silk-road.md)

## Medieval Period (476 - 1500)

### 700-1200 - Islamic Golden Age
- **Al-Khwarizmi** develops algebra (essential for finance)
- **Islamic banking** innovations: partnerships (mudaraba), profit-sharing
- Prohibition of riba (usury) shapes alternative finance structures
- See: [Islamic Golden Age Finance](ancient/islamic-banking.md)

### 1000-1500 - Medieval Trade Fairs
- Champagne fairs become commercial hubs
- Letters of credit, bills of exchange emerge
- Merchant guilds establish trade standards
- See: [Trade Fairs & Merchant Guilds](medieval-renaissance/trade-fairs.md)

### 1100s - Scholastic Economics
- **Thomas Aquinas** develops "just price" theory
- Debates on usury, private property, economic justice
- See: [Scholastic Economics](medieval-renaissance/scholastic-economics.md)

### 1397 - Medici Bank Founded
- Giovanni di Bicci de' Medici establishes bank in Florence
- Innovations in branch banking, double-entry bookkeeping
- Finances Renaissance art and papal operations
- See: [Italian Banking Houses](medieval-renaissance/italian-banking.md), [The Medici Family](figures/medici.md)

### 1494 - Pacioli's *Summa de Arithmetica*
- **Luca Pacioli** publishes first printed work on double-entry bookkeeping
- "Venetian method" becomes accounting standard
- See: [Double-Entry Bookkeeping](medieval-renaissance/double-entry.md)

## Stagflation & Neoliberal Turn (1970s - 1990s)

### 1971 - Nixon Shock âš¡
- President Nixon ends dollar-gold convertibility (Aug 15)
- Bretton Woods collapses, fiat currency era begins
- See: [The Nixon Shock](central-banking/nixon-shock.md)

### 1973-1979 - Stagflation Crisis âš¡
- Oil shocks, high inflation + high unemployment
- Keynesian Phillips curve breaks down
- See: [Stagflation](crises/stagflation.md)

### 1976 - Hayek Wins Nobel Prize
- Recognition of Austrian School contributions
- Critique of Keynesianism gains traction

### 1979-1982 - Volcker Shock
- Fed Chairman Paul Volcker raises rates to 20%
- Breaks inflation but causes severe recession
- Monetarist experiment

### 1980s - Rise of Financial Derivatives
- Futures, swaps, exotic options proliferate
- Deregulation accelerates (Reagan/Thatcher era)
- See: [Financial Derivatives](modern-finance/derivatives.md)

### 1986 - Hyman Minsky's Financial Instability Hypothesis
- **Post-Keynesian** critique: stability breeds instability
- Hedge, speculative, Ponzi finance stages
- Predicts 2008 crisis dynamics

### 1990s - Cypherpunk Movement
- **Timothy May**: *Crypto Anarchist Manifesto* (1988)

- **Nick Szabo**: bit gold concept (1998)
- See: [Cypherpunk Movement](crypto-future/cypherpunks.md)

### 1992 - European Exchange Rate Mechanism Crisis
